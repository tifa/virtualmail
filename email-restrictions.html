Email Restrictions

At the moment sender/recipient policies are managed through direct sqlite querying, so here’s a cheatsheet.

Reject Senders

Reject incoming mail from specific email addresses with a REJECT response.

INSERT INTO reject_senders (username, domain_name, description)
VALUES ('user1', 'example.org', 'Spamming me');

Reject Recipients

Reject incoming mail to specific email addresses with a REJECT response. These take precedence over any catch-alls in the users table.

INSERT INTO reject_recipients (username, domain_id, memo)
SELECT 'user1', domain_id, 'Company had a data leak'
FROM `domains`
WHERE domain_name = 'example.com';
